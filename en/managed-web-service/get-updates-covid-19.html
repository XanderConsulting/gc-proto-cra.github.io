Sign up to “Get COVID-19 Updates” from the Government of Canada – (Step 1 of 3)

You are signing up to receive updates from the Government of Canada about Coronavirus (COVID-19).

Our Service

We will only use your email address to send you trusted information about COVID-19. We will not share your email address.

Emails from "Get Updates on COVID-19" will come from get.covid19.updates.obtenir.des.nouvelles.de. covid19@notification.alpha.canada.ca. The email will never ask for personal information.

The service is opt-in. Only people who want updates on the COVID-19 situation in Canada will receive the emails.

If you decide not to sign up, you will still receive emergency notifications on your mobile phone. This email service is in addition to existing provincial and territorial emergency alerts, like Alert Ready text notifications or "Amber Alerts".

Your email address is being collected under Section 4 of the Department of Health Act and it will be protected according to the Privacy Act. If you have any questions about your privacy, please contact Health Canada Privacy Management Division at: hc.privacy-vie.privee.sc@canada.ca.

How to Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe from this list at any time - there is an unsubscribe link in every email. Your email address will be deleted when this service ends.
